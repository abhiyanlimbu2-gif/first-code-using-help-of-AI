About MeroRide

A simple platform for bike rentals and basic management.

Who We Are

MeroRide is a bike renting system developed to provide a basic and easy-to-use platform for renting bikes. The system focuses on simplicity and accessibility rather than complex features.

What We Do

MeroRide allows users to view available bikes and rent them for a specific period of time. It also provides basic administrative functionality to manage bike and user information.

Our Approach

We aim to keep the system straightforward and practical. MeroRide is designed as a foundational platform that can be improved and expanded with additional features in the future.

Simple Design

A clean and minimal interface that is easy to understand and use.

Basic Rental Flow

Users can rent bikes using a simple and clear process.

Secure Platform

User data and rental information are managed securely.
